Experiment instructions

Before you start this experiment, please read carefully the following:
This experiment consists of a learning phase and a testing phase.
In the learning phase:
You will hear the sounds of some Korean words and see the picture representation of their meanings. You will have 5 seconds to learn each word.
In the testing phase:
You will hear those words again in a random order. You will then need to select the corresponding picture according to the word you hear.
Please try your best to remember as many words as you can.
Now click the button below when you are ready.
